Data source and design notes

Built to expand beyond New York later by swapping in new state configs and geography joins.

  • County crime rates and population: NYS Open Data dataset 34dd-6g2j
  • County total offense categories: NYS Open Data dataset ca8h-8gjq
  • County boundaries for the map: us-atlas TopoJSON, derived from U.S. Census geography

Notes: overall index crime is the official top-line county metric across reported index offenses. The crime impact score is a site-defined weighted lens, not an official NYS field: murder 25, rape 12, robbery 6, aggravated assault 4, burglary 2, motor vehicle theft 2, larceny 1.

New York City boroughs appear as counties because the official source is county-based. Rates are shown per 100,000 residents. ZIP-level exploration can be added later if a trustworthy, statewide, official source becomes available.
